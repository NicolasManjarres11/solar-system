Solar System

The Solar System is made up of a medium-sized central star (the Sun), its accompanying bodies (planets, comets, satellites, meteoroids, interplanetary gas and dust) and the space between them. The Solar System originated about 4.5 billion years ago, from a rotating gas cloud.
